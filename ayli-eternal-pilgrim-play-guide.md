# Ayli, Eternal Pilgrim — Play Guide

**Format:** Commander | **Colors:** Orzhov (BW) | **Deck Size:** 100

## Strategy

Life gain + sacrifice. Gain life to activate Ayli's exile ability (cost: sacrifice a creature with total power 10+, you gain life equal to its toughness). Drain opponents with Blood Artist, Vito, and Zulaport Cutthroat when creatures die. Use sacrifice outlets to turn your creatures into value and damage.

## How to Win

1. **Drain and pain** — Blood Artist + Vito + Zulaport Cutthroat drain opponents whenever creatures die
2. **Ayli exile** — Once you have 50+ life, sacrifice any creature to permanently exile a threat
3. **Exsanguinate** — Late-game X spell that drains all opponents for massive life swing
4. **Massacre Wurm** — Punishes creature-heavy opponents and drains on death
5. **Kambal, Profiteering Mayor** — Drains opponents whenever they cast noncreature spells

## Early Game (Turns 1–4)

**Priority: Establish life gain engine and sacrifice outlets.**

| Turn | Play |
|------|------|
| 1 | Sol Ring, Healer's Hawk, or Viscera Seer |
| 2 | Ajani's Pridemate, Blood Artist, or Sign in Blood |
| 3 | Cast Ayli, or play Vampire Nighthawk / Sun-Blessed Healer |
| 4 | Sacrifice outlet (Vampiric Rites, Skullclamp) + life gain creature |

Key early plays:
- **Blood Artist** or **Zulaport Cutthroat** before any board interaction — they punish opponents for creature deaths
- **Viscera Seer** — free sacrifice outlet that also filters your draws
- **Ajani's Pridemate** — grows every time you gain life, becomes a big threat fast
- **Defile** — 1-mana removal that scales with swamps

## Mid Game (Turns 5–7)

**Priority: Build life total and start controlling the board.**

- Cast Ayli and start sacrificing creatures for value
- **Vito, Thorn of the Dusk Rose** — every life gain event also drains an opponent
- **Marauding Blight-Priest** — gains life and drains on creature deaths
- **Phyrexian Arena** — steady card draw (the life loss is offset by your gain)
- **Mentor of the Meek** — draw cards whenever a creature enters (great with tokens)
- **Kalastria Highborn** — pay 1 when a vampire dies to drain 2 and gain 2

## Late Game (Turns 8+)

**Priority: Close out the game with drain effects and Ayli's exile.**

- **Exsanguinate** for X=10+ — massive life swing that can kill opponents outright
- **Ayli's exile ability** — once at 50+ life, sacrifice any creature to permanently remove any nonland permanent
- **Massacre Wurm** — wipe out small creatures and drain for each one that dies
- **Sun Titan** — recur key creatures and enchantments from graveyard every turn
- **Lurrus of the Dream-Den** — cast 2-mana spells from graveyard for recurring value

## Key Combos

| Combo | How It Works |
|-------|-------------|
| Ayli + Viscera Seer | Sacrifice creatures to scry + trigger Ayli's exile at 50+ life |
| Blood Artist + Vampiric Rites | Sacrifice a creature: drain 1, draw a card, gain 1 life |
| Vito + any life gain | Every life gain event also drains an opponent for the same amount |
| Skullclamp + any 1-toughness creature | Draw 2 cards for 1 mana (equips to Blood Artist, Viscera Seer, etc.) |
| Kambal + opponent's noncreature spell | They lose 2 life, you gain 2 life |
| Corrupted Conviction / Village Rites + dying creature | Sacrifice in response to death: draw 2 cards |
| Felidar Retreat + land drop | Create a 2/2 cat or put +1/+1 counters on your team |
| Braids, Arisen Nightmare | Forces sacrifices — your creatures dying triggers Blood Artist/Vito |

## Card Roles

### Life Gain Engine
- **Ayli, Eternal Pilgrim** — Commander. Sacrifice outlet that gains life; at 50+ life, exiles any nonland permanent
- **Vito, Thorn of the Dusk Rose** — Turns all life gain into opponent drain
- **Ajani's Pridemate** — Grows with every life gain event
- **Linden, the Steadfast Queen** — Gains life whenever attacking creatures deal combat damage
- **Sun-Blessed Healer** — Gains 1 life whenever you cast a spell
- **Angel of Vitality** — Buffs all your life gain by 1
- **Marauding Blight-Priest** — Gains life and drains on creature deaths
- **Vampire Nighthawk** — Flying, deathtouch, lifelink — does everything
- **Child of Night** — Cheap lifelink creature
- **Basilisk Collar** — Gives any creature deathtouch + lifelink

### Sacrifice Outlets & Death Triggers
- **Viscera Seer** — Free sacrifice + scry
- **Blood Artist** — Drains 1 and gains 1 on any creature death
- **Zulaport Cutthroat** — Same as Blood Artist but only your creatures
- **Vampiric Rites** — Pay 1, sacrifice, draw a card, gain 1 life
- **Skullclamp** — Draw 2 cards when equipped creature dies
- **Braids, Arisen Nightmare** — Forces sacrifices each turn, draws you cards
- **Village Rites / Corrupted Conviction / Deadly Dispute** — Instant-speed sacrifice + draw

### Removal
- **Path to Exile** — 1-mana exile removal
- **Anguished Unmaking** — Exile any nonland permanent (3 life is fine in this deck)
- **Mortify** — Destroy creature or enchantment
- **Hero's Downfall** — Destroy creature or planeswalker
- **Doom Blade** — Cheap creature removal
- **Grasp of Darkness** — -4/-4 kills most creatures
- **Feed the Swarm** — Destroy creature or enchantment, lose life equal to CMC
- **Defile** — 1-mana removal that scales with swamps
- **Wrath of God** — Board wipe (your death triggers still fire)
- **Massacre Wurm** — Creature wipe + drain
- **Stroke of Midnight** — Exile any nonland permanent (creates a 2/2 for opponent, but you can remove it)

### Card Draw
- **Phyrexian Arena** — 1 extra card per turn (life loss offset by gain)
- **Sign in Blood** — 2 cards for 2 life
- **Mentor of the Meek** — Draw whenever a creature enters
- **Inspiring Overseer** — Flying body + draw on entry
- **Midnight Reaper** — Draw whenever a nontoken creature dies
- **Mazemind Tome** — Flexible draw/scry

### Recursion
- **Sun Titan** — Recurs any permanent with CMC 3 or less every turn
- **Cadaver Imp** — Flying body that recurs a creature
- **Sevinne's Reclamation** — Recurs a permanent from graveyard
- **Lurrus of the Dream-Den** — Cast 2-mana permanents from graveyard
- **Macabre Waltz** — Return 2 creatures from graveyard, discard 1

## Mulligan Guide

**Keep:** 2–3 lands, at least 1 removal spell, preferably a life gain creature or sacrifice outlet

**Mulligan if:**
- All lands, no early plays
- All expensive cards with no ramp
- No removal and no life gain creatures

## Weaknesses

- **Enchantment removal** can take out Phyrexian Arena and Felidar Retreat
- **Graveyard hate** shuts down Sun Titan, Cadaver Imp, and Lurrus recursion
- **Anti-life-gain** (Erebos, Tainted Remedy) directly counters the strategy
- **Exile removal** bypasses death triggers — your Blood Artists won't fire
- **Fast aggro** can outrace you before you establish the life gain engine

## Land Notes

- 11 Plains, 10 Swamp — slightly white-heavy for the life gain creatures
- **Scoured Barrens** — enters tapped but gains 1 life (triggers Vito/Pridemate)
- **Temple of Silence** — enters tapped, scry 1 (helps find answers)
- **Krosan Verge** — finds dual lands from library (can find dual lands with white/black)
- **Shefet Dunes** — pay life to buff team (you have plenty of life to spend)
- **Cryptic Caves** — Draw a card when you have excess lands
