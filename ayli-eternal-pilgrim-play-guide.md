## 🎴 Quick Reference — Cheat Sheet

> 🎯 **Your Game Plan:** Gain life → trigger drain → exile threats at 50+ life → close with Exsanguinate or board wipes.

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **1. Mulligan** | Keep 2-3 lands + 1 removal + lifegain/sac outlet |
| **2. Early Game** | Play `Hinterland Sanctifier` or `Blood Artist` first |
| **3. Mid Game** | Set up `Vito` + `Rhox Faithmender`; control the board |
| **4. Late Game** | `Exsanguinate` for X=8+ or `Kindred Dominance` to wipe & drain |
| **5. Win Con** | Drain opponents out with death triggers & lifegain |

> ⚡ **Always Remember:**
> - `Hinterland Sanctifier` is your **MVP 1-drop** — it triggers on every creature you cast.
> - `Kindred Dominance` naming **Cleric** is your most powerful reset button.
> - The **Fiendish Panda Loop** (equip `Skullclamp`, sac to `Viscera Seer`, Panda returns) draws 2 cards and drains every turn.
> - **50+ life** = `Ayli`'s exile ability turns every creature into permanent removal.

## 📖 Strategy Overview

This is an **Orzhov lifegain & sacrifice** deck. Your core loop is simple but devastating:

1. **Gain life** through creatures, spells, and lifelink.
2. **Trigger drain** with `Vito`, `Marauding Blight-Priest`, and death effects.
3. **Sacrifice creatures** for value using outlets like `Viscera Seer` and `Vampiric Rites`.
4. **Exile threats** once `Ayli` reaches 50+ life.
5. **Close the game** with `Exsanguinate`, `Kindred Dominance`, or overwhelming drain.

> 🕊️ *"The path to victory is paved in life — and your opponents' creatures."*

## 🌅 Early Game (Turns 1–4)

> **Priority:** Establish your lifegain engine and sacrifice outlets.

| Turn | Ideal Play |
|------|-----------|
| **1** | `Sol Ring`, `Viscera Seer`, `Hinterland Sanctifier`, or `Duress` |
| **2** | `Blood Artist`, `Ajani's Pridemate`, or `Sign in Blood` |
| **3** | Cast `Ayli`, or play `Vampire Nighthawk` / `Vito` |
| **4** | Sacrifice outlet (`Vampiric Rites`, `Skullclamp`) + lifegain creature |

### ⭐ Key Early Plays

> 🎯 **Blood Artist** or **Zulaport Cutthroat** — Play these *before* any board interaction. They punish opponents for every creature death.

- **Viscera Seer** — Free sac outlet that filters your draws.
- **Ajani's Pridemate** — Grows every time you gain life. Becomes a massive threat fast.
- **Hinterland Sanctifier** — The **MVP 1-drop**. Gains 1 life on every creature ETB, chaining into `Vito`, `Pridemate`, and `Blight-Priest`.
- **Moment of Craving** — Removal *plus* 2 life for 2 mana.

---

## ⚔️ Mid Game (Turns 5–7)

> **Priority:** Build your life total and start controlling the board.

- Cast `Ayli` and begin sacrificing creatures for value.
- **Vito, Thorn of the Dusk Rose** — Every lifegain event also drains an opponent.
- **Rhox Faithmender** — Doubles **ALL** lifegain (`Exsanguinate`, `Ayli` sacs, lifelink — everything).
- **Marauding Blight-Priest** — Every lifegain event drains **each opponent** for 1.
- **Phyrexian Arena** — Steady card draw. The life loss is easily offset by your gain.
- **Mentor of the Meek** — Draw cards whenever a creature enters (amazing with tokens).
- **Kalastria Highborn** — Pay 1 when a Vampire dies to drain 2 and gain 2.
- **Fiendish Panda** — When it dies, reanimates a creature. Key loop piece with `Skullclamp`/`Viscera Seer` (sac → draw 2 → Panda returns → repeat).
- **Cat Collector** — Creates 1/1 Cat tokens on your end step (free sac fodder for `Blood Artist`/`Vito`).
- **Nullpriest of Oblivion** — Lifelink + menace; kicked, reanimates a creature.
- **Midnight Snack** — Creates 2/2 Zombie tokens; sacrifice to drain opponents for all life gained this turn.

---

## 👑 Late Game (Turns 8+)

> **Priority:** Close out the game with drain effects and Ayli's exile.

- **Exsanguinate** for X=8+ — Doubled by `Rhox Faithmender`, this drains opponents for **16+ life each**.
- **Ayli's exile ability** — At 50+ life, sac any creature to permanently exile any nonland permanent.
- **Massacre Wurm** — Wipes small creatures and drains for each one that dies.
- **Kindred Dominance** — Name **Cleric** to keep `Hinterland Sanctifier` and `Vito` (Vampire Cleric) while destroying everything else. This is your most powerful reset.
- **Sun Titan** — Recur key creatures and enchantments from graveyard every turn.
- **Fumigate** — Board wipe that gains life for each creature destroyed (triggers `Vito` if alive).

> ⚠️ **Critical Timing:** Cast `Fumigate` when you have `Blood Artist`/`Vito` out. You wipe the board, drain for each death, *and* gain life from Fumigate itself.

| Keep | Mulligan |

## ⚠️ Weaknesses

> 🚨 *Know your vulnerabilities. These are the decks and effects that can shut you down.*

| Threat | Why It Hurts | Counterplay |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Fast draw-damage** (`Nekusar`, `Underworld Dreams`, `Spiteful Visions`) | Your lifegain can't keep up with 3+ damage per draw | Remove `Underworld Dreams`/`Spiteful Visions` immediately. Race to 7 mana for `Kindred Dominance` |
| **Enchantment removal** | Takes out `Phyrexian Arena` and `Midnight Snack` | Protect key enchantments; have backup draw |
| **Graveyard hate** | Shuts down `Sun Titan`, `Cadaver Imp`, `Fiendish Panda` recursion | Use `Soul-Guide Lantern` proactively |
| **Anti-lifegain** (`Erebos`, `Tainted Remedy`) | Directly counters your entire strategy | Remove immediately with `Anguished Unmaking` or `Path to Exile` |
| **Exile removal** | Bypasses death triggers — `Blood Artist` won't fire | Accept the loss; focus on lifegain drain instead |
| **Phyrexian Arena at low life** | Can kill you if below 5 life | Don't hesitate to `Disenchant` your own Arena |

> ⚠️ **Hardest Matchup:** `Nekusar` style wheel decks. If they resolve `Underworld Dreams` + `Spiteful Visions` + a wheel by Turn 6-7, you may die before reaching 7 mana for `Kindred Dominance`.

## 🎓 Game Simulation Insights

> 📊 *10 games played — **70% Win Rate** (7 wins)*

### Top 10 Lessons Learned

1. 🏆 **Kindred Dominance is the deck's most powerful card** — cast in 7/10 games; decisive in every win. Naming Cleric keeps `Hinterland Sanctifier` and `Vito`. The drain triggers often deal **15-20+ damage**.

2. 🐼 **The Fiendish Panda Loop is your best value engine** — equip `Skullclamp`, sac to `Viscera Seer`, Panda returns itself. Each iteration: draw 2, drain each opponent for 2+, gain 1 life. Repeat every turn.

3. 🩸 **Rhox Faithmender + Exsanguinate is the strongest finisher** — X=8 drains 8 from each opponent and gains you 16 life (doubled).

4. ⏰ **Fumigate timing is critical** — cast it with `Blood Artist`/`Vito` out to drain for each death, then gain life from Fumigate itself.

5. ✝️ **The 50+ life exile ability is game-ending** — once online, systematically remove opponents' key permanents every turn.

6. ⚖️ **Phyrexian Arena is a double-edged sword** — it draws you out of trouble but can kill you at low life. Don't be afraid to `Disenchant` your own Arena.

7. 🤝 **Political play matters** — appearing less threatening while building the drain engine is optimal in multiplayer.

8. 💀 **Nekusar is your hardest matchup** — the only deck that consistently beats you. Prioritize removing `Underworld Dreams` and `Spiteful Visions` immediately.

9. 🍫 **Midnight Snack is a late-game nuke** — after a turn of heavy lifegain (Ayli sacs, lifelink attacks, Sanctifier triggers), saccing it can drain an opponent for **8-12+ life** out of nowhere.

10. 🌟 **Hinterland Sanctifier is the MVP early play** — at 1 mana, it triggers on every creature cast, chaining into `Vito`/`Pridemate`/`Blight-Priest`. It's the card that makes the whole engine hum.
