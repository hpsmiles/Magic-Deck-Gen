# Omnath, Locus of Rage — Play Guide

**Format:** Commander | **Colors:** Gruul (GR) | **Deck Size:** 100

## Strategy

Landfall elementals. Ramp aggressively, play extra lands per turn, and trigger Omnath's landfall ability to create 5/5 Elemental tokens. When elementals die, Omnath deals 3 damage to any target. Sacrifice outlets let you turn elementals into direct damage on demand.

## How to Win

1. **Landfall swarm** — Drop lands, make 5/5 elementals, overwhelm with numbers
2. **Omnath damage** — Sacrifice elementals via Goblin Bombardment or combat to ping opponents for 3 each
3. **Scute Swarm exponential** — With extra land drops, Scute Swarm creates an army that doubles each landfall
4. **Impact Tremors burn** — Every creature that enters deals 1 damage; combined with token generators, this adds up fast

## Early Game (Turns 1–4)

**Priority: Ramp and set up extra land drops.**

| Turn | Play |
|------|------|
| 1 | Llanowar Elves, Sol Ring, or Evolving Wilds (triggers landfall) |
| 2 | Ramp spell (Explore, Rampant Growth) or Lotus Cobra + land drop |
| 3 | Extra land enabler (Azusa, Mina and Denn) or ramp (Circuitous Route) |
| 4 | Cast Omnath if you have 5 mana, or continue ramping |

Key early plays:
- **Lotus Cobra** + fetchland/Evolving Wilds = burst mana
- **Crop Rotation** at end of turn to find Sheltered Thicket or a utility land
- **Expedition Map** to find the land you need
- **Green Sun's Zenith** for X=0 to find a landfall creature like Elvish Reclaimer

## Mid Game (Turns 5–7)

**Priority: Cast Omnath and start generating elementals.**

- Cast Omnath and play lands to create 5/5 elementals
- **Scute Swarm** with extra land drops creates an exponential army
- **Patient Naturalist** and **Ramunap Excavator** let you replay lands from graveyard
- **Impact Tremors** before casting Omnath = damage for each elemental created
- **Dragon Fodder** and **Krenko, Tin Street Kingpin** create tokens that trigger Impact Tremors
- **Shigeki, Jukai Visionary** — bounce to hand, reveal top 4, put a land onto the battlefield (repeatable landfall)
- **Viridian Emissary** — chump block or sacrifice, then tutor a land (landfall trigger + death trigger)

## Late Game (Turns 8+)

**Priority: Overwhelm with landfall triggers and sacrifice outlets.**

- **Avenger of Zendikar** + land drop = massive plant army
- **Rampaging Baloths** creates 4/4 beasts on every landfall
- **Ancient Greenwarden** doubles all your landfall triggers
- **Goblin Bombardment** turns elementals into direct damage — sacrifice an elemental: 3 damage from Omnath + 1 from Bombardment = 4 damage per creature
- **Hour of Promise** finds 2 lands (2 landfall triggers) + zombie tokens for sacrifice
- **Nahiri's Lithoforming** — sacrifice lands to draw cards, then replay them for double landfall triggers
- **Dance of the Tumbleweeds** — tutor a land (landfall) + create an elemental token (sacrifice fodder)

## Key Combos

| Combo | How It Works |
|-------|-------------|
| Omnath + Goblin Bombardment | Sacrifice elementals for 4 damage each (3 Omnath + 1 Bombardment) |
| Omnath + Skullclamp | Sacrifice elementals to draw 2 cards + deal 3 damage |
| Scute Swarm + Azusa/Oracle | Extra land drops create exponentially more insects |
| Ancient Greenwarden + any landfall | Doubles all landfall triggers |
| Impact Tremors + Omnath | Each elemental created deals 1 additional damage |
| Crop Rotation + Lotus Cobra | Fetch a land, Cobra triggers for extra mana |
| Ramunap Excavator + fetchlands | Replay Evolving Wilds/Terramorphic Expanse from graveyard |
| Nahiri's Lithoforming + Omnath | Sacrifice lands to draw, replay them for double landfall triggers |
| Elvish Reclaimer + Omnath | Sacrifice a land to find another = landfall trigger on demand |
| Green Sun's Zenith | Tutors for any green landfall creature at any stage |

## Card Roles

### Landfall Payoffs
- **Omnath, Locus of Rage** — Commander. Creates 5/5 elementals on landfall, deals 3 damage when they die
- **Scute Swarm** — Creates a copy of itself on landfall; with extra drops, grows exponentially
- **Rampaging Baloths** — 4/4 beast on every landfall
- **Avenger of Zendikar** — Creates plant tokens, then buffs them on landfall
- **Ancient Greenwarden** — Doubles all landfall triggers; lets you play lands from graveyard
- **Titania, Protector of Argoth** — 5/3 elementals when lands go to graveyard
- **Patient Naturalist** — Explores on landfall (draw or land to hand)
- **Nesting Dragon** — Creates 2/2 dragon tokens on landfall
- **World Shaper** — Returns all lands from graveyard to battlefield when it dies

### Extra Land Drops & Land Tutors
- **Azusa, Lost but Seeking** — Play 2 additional lands per turn
- **Oracle of Mul Daya** — Play additional land from top of library
- **Mina and Denn, Wildborn** — Additional land drop + land bounce for re-triggering
- **Wayward Swordtooth** — Additional land drop (city's blessing required)
- **Ramunap Excavator** — Play lands from graveyard (replay fetchlands)
- **Shigeki, Jukai Visionary** — Bounce to hand, reveal top 4, put a land onto the battlefield (repeatable)
- **Elvish Reclaimer** — Sacrifice a land to find any land (landfall trigger on demand)
- **Viridian Emissary** — When it dies, tutor a basic land (sacrifice synergy + landfall)
- **Hour of Promise** — Find 2 lands + zombie tokens (2 landfall triggers)
- **Dance of the Tumbleweeds** — Tutor a land + create elemental token
- **Nahiri's Lithoforming** — Sacrifice lands to draw, then replay for double landfall

### Sacrifice Outlets
- **Goblin Bombardment** — Free sacrifice outlet, deals 1 damage
- **Skullclamp** — Draw 2 cards when equipped creature dies
- **Vampiric Rites** — Pay 1, sacrifice a creature, gain 1 life and draw a card

### Ramp
- **Llanowar Elves** — 1-mana dork
- **Lotus Cobra** — Burst mana on land drops
- **Bloom Tender** — Taps for G or R (or both with Omnath out)
- **Circuitous Route, Grow from the Ashes, Map the Frontier** — Land ramp spells
- **Green Sun's Zenith** — Tutors for any green creature (finds landfall creatures at any stage)

## Mulligan Guide

**Keep:** 2–3 lands, at least 1 ramp spell, preferably a landfall enabler or Lotus Cobra

**Mulligan if:**
- All lands, no ramp or action
- All spells, 0–1 lands
- Too many expensive cards with no ramp

## Weaknesses

- **Graveyard hate** shuts down Ramunap Excavator, Life from the Loam, World Shaper, and Shigeki
- **Board wipes** kill your elemental army (but each death triggers Omnath damage)
- **Enchantment removal** can take out Impact Tremors and your key enchantments
- **Counter spells** on Omnath sets you back significantly — use bait spells first

## Land Notes

- 12 Forest, 10 Mountain — slightly green-heavy to support the ramp package
- **Evolving Wilds / Terramorphic Expanse** — trigger landfall twice (crack + the land that enters)
- **Sheltered Thicket, Rugged Highlands** — dual lands that can be found with ramp spells
- **Crawling Barrens** — mana sink for late game, becomes a big threat
- **Ramunap Ruins** — damage in long games
