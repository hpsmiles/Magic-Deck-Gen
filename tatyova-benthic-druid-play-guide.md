# Tatyova, Benthic Druid — Play Guide

**Format:** Commander | **Colors:** Simic (GU) | **Deck Size:** 100

## Strategy

Landfall draw. Play extra lands per turn to trigger Tatyova — every land that enters draws you a card and gains 1 life. Ramp aggressively, control the board with blue counters and bounce, then overwhelm with landfall triggers and big creatures.

## How to Win

1. **Card advantage engine** — Tatyova draws a card for every land drop; with extra land effects, you draw 2-3 cards per turn
2. **Landfall swarm** — Rampaging Baloths and Turntimber Sower create armies on landfall
3. **Rite of Replication** — Kicked on Rampaging Baloths = 20 4/4 beasts + 5 landfall triggers
4. **Big blue finishers** — Prime Speaker Zegana draws 10+ cards, Steel Hellkite removes threats
5. **River's Rebuke** — Mass bounce clears opponents' boards while you rebuild faster with land ramp

## Early Game (Turns 1–4)

**Priority: Ramp and set up card draw.**

| Turn | Play |
|------|------|
| 1 | Llanowar Elves, Ponder, or Opt |
| 2 | Ramp spell (Harrow, Springbloom Druid) or cantrip (Preordain) |
| 3 | More ramp (Circuitous Route, Grow from the Ashes) or Tatyova if you have 5 mana |
| 4 | Cast Tatyova if you haven't yet, or hold up counterspells |

Key early plays:
- **Ponder / Opt / Preordain** — smooth your draws and find lands
- **Harrow** at instant speed — sacrifice a land, get 2 basics (2 Tatyova triggers)
- **Hedron Crab** on turn 1 — mills 3 per landfall (fuels graveyard strategies)

## Mid Game (Turns 5–7)

**Priority: Cast Tatyova and start drawing extra cards.**

- Cast Tatyova and every land drop draws a card + gains 1 life
- **Rampaging Baloths** — 4/4 beast on every landfall
- **Circuitous Route** — finds 2 gates = 2 Tatyova triggers
- **Dictate of Kruphix** — everyone draws extra, but you draw even more from landfall
- **Hedron Archive** — ramp now, sac for 2 cards later
- Hold up **Negate** and **Mana Leak** to protect Tatyova

## Late Game (Turns 8+)

**Priority: Overwhelm with card advantage and landfall triggers.**

- **Rite of Replication** kicked on Rampaging Baloths = 5 copies + 5 landfall triggers = 20 beasts
- **Prime Speaker Zegana** — draws cards equal to the greatest power on the board (often 6+)
- **River's Rebuke** — bounce all nonland permanents of one player
- **Zopandrel, Hunger Dominus** — doubles all your creatures' power and toughness
- **Primeval Bounty** — land = 3/3 beast, creature = 3 life, spell = 1/1 plant

|-------|-------------|
| Tatyova + Harrow | Instant speed: sacrifice a land, get 2 basics = draw 2 cards + gain 2 life |
| Tatyova + Evolving Wilds | Crack for 2 triggers (sacrifice + land that enters) |

| Tatyova + Dictate of Kruphix | Extra draw step + land drop draw = massive card advantage |

| Talrand + any instant/sorcery | Creates 2/2 drake for every spell you cast |

## Card Roles

### Landfall Payoffs
- **Tatyova, Benthic Druid** — Commander. Draw a card + gain 1 life on every landfall
- **Rampaging Baloths** — 4/4 beast on every landfall
- **Hedron Crab** — Mills 3 on every landfall (graveyard fuel)
- **Turntimber Sower** — 1/1 plant on every landfall
- **Patient Naturalist** — Explores on landfall (draw or land to hand)

### Extra Land Drops & Ramp
- **Harrow** — Instant speed, 2 lands (2 Tatyova triggers)
- **Circuitous Route** — 2 gates to battlefield
- **Springbloom Druid** — Land to battlefield
- **Map the Frontier** — 2 basic lands
- **Grow from the Ashes** — Kicker for 2 lands
- **Dance of the Tumbleweeds** — Land tutor + elemental token
- **Eccentric Farmer / Satyr Wayfinder** — Mill + may get land
- **Ordeal of Nylea** — Attack counters + 2 lands when sacrificed

### Card Draw
- **Ponder / Opt / Preordain** — 1-mana cantrips to smooth draws
- **Chart a Course** — Draw 2 for 2 mana
- **Think Twice** — Draw with flashback (2 cards from 1 spell)
- **Dictate of Kruphix** — Howling Mine effect (you benefit most from landfall draw)
- **Spectral Sailor** — Flying + card draw ability
- **Garruk's Packleader** — Draw on power 3+ creatures entering
- **Finale of Revelation** — Big draw spell that scales
- **Thopter Spy Network** — Draw on combat damage + thopters

### Control & Interaction
- **Negate / Mana Leak / Cancel / Essence Scatter** — Counterspells
- **Aetherize** — Bounce all attacking creatures
- **Into the Roil** — Bounce + draw
- **Slip Out the Back / March of Swirling Mist** — Phase out (protection + removal)
- **Unsummon** — Cheap bounce
- **Naturalize** — Artifact/enchantment removal
- **River's Rebuke** — Mass bounce of one player's nonland permanents

## Mulligan Guide

**Keep:** 2–3 lands, at least 1 cantrip or ramp spell, preferably a counterspell

**Mulligan if:**
- All lands, no action
- All expensive cards with no ramp
- No blue sources for counterspells

## Weaknesses

- **Tatyova removal** — she's the engine; without her, the deck is just ramp. Protect her with Snakeskin Veil and counterspells
- **Fast aggro** — the deck is slow to develop a board; Aetherize and Fog Bank help stall
- **Graveyard hate** — shuts down Hedron Crab, Mending of Dominaria, and Satyr Wayfinder
- **Anti-draw** — Narset, Notion Rain effects punish your card advantage strategy

## Land Notes

- 14 Forest, 12 Island — green-heavy for the ramp package
- **Evolving Wilds / Terramorphic Expanse** — 2 Tatyova triggers per crack
- **Temple of Mystery** — scry 1 + GU dual land
- **Botanical Sanctum** — fast land, enters untapped early
- **Krosan Verge** — finds Forest + Island (perfect for this deck)
- **Maze's End** — alt wincon with gate package, finds gates on activation
